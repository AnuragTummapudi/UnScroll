# UnScroll

UnScroll is a smart screen time reduction app designed to help users regain control over their digital habits. By providing a suite of tools and unique features, UnScroll encourages mindful device usage, reduces impulsive scrolling, and promotes a healthier balance between online and offline life.

---

## Features

UnScroll combines proven strategies and innovative ideas to help you reduce screen time effectively:

1. **App Usage Tracking** – Monitor daily screen time and usage patterns.  
2. **Customizable Time Limits** – Set personalized limits for social media, games, and other apps.  
3. **Focus Mode** – Temporarily block distracting apps to improve concentration.  
4. **Pomodoro Timer** – Work in focused intervals with breaks to maintain productivity.  
5. **Usage Reminders** – Gentle notifications when approaching app time limits.  
6. **Time Tax** – A unique “tax” system that penalizes excessive screen use to encourage discipline.
7. **Daily and Weekly Reports** – Visualize your progress with detailed analytics.
8. **Offline Mode** – App functionality even without internet access.
9. **Screen-Free Rewards** – Earn rewards and achievements for meeting goals.
10. **Profile & Settings Sync** – Sync your settings and progress across devices.
11. **Emergency Access** – Unlock apps temporarily for urgent needs with confirmation.
12. **Mood Tracker** – Track your emotions and correlate with screen time.
13. **Digital Wellbeing Dashboard** – Comprehensive insights and tips for mindful use.
14. \* **Mindful Popups with 3D Character** – Friendly 3D character popups that greet and prompt you to take a break when opening social media or games, inspired by gamified apps like Duolingo.
15. \* **Focus Music & Ambient Sounds** – Enhance concentration with curated sounds.
16. \* **Friend Network** – Connect, compete, and grow with friends.
17. \* **Social Media Lockout** – Automatic locking of distracting apps outside permitted times.
18. \* **Personalised Prompts and Challenges** – Personalized messages and mini-challenges to motivate you.
